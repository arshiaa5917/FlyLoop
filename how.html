How FlyLoop Works

This page explains the flow of a travel subscription like FlyLoop from a customer’s point of view. You can describe the model here for your portfolio or pitch.

The basic journey

  • Choose a membership tier (Basic, Standard, or Premium).
  • Pay a fixed monthly subscription instead of random large bills.
  • Build eligibility or “trip credits” as you stay subscribed.
  • When you’re ready, pick one of the available destinations.
  • FlyLoop coordinates flights, hotels and suggested itineraries.

Why it’s attractive

  • Predictable cost instead of fluctuating flight prices.
  • Less emotional friction: travel becomes part of routine.
  • Curated destinations — no need to research everything alone.
  • Room for loyalty perks, pause options, and credit systems.
Business-side notes (for you)

In a real implementation, FlyLoop would use minimum commitments, credits that unlock trips, and loyalty rewards to prevent churn and abuse like “join → travel → cancel”. This page is a perfect place to summarize those mechanisms for demos or investors.
