Frequently Asked Questions

This page is written like a real product FAQ, but it also doubles as a way to explain your concept clearly to anyone viewing your portfolio.

Is FlyLoop live right now?
No. This website is a concept and portfolio piece that shows how a subscription-based travel service could look and feel. It’s useful for user testing, business planning and investor pitches.
Would flights and hotels actually be included?
In a real version, yes. Each plan would include flights, hotels, basic transfers and curated suggestions, negotiated using wholesale travel rates and partner agreements.
How do you stop people from joining, traveling once, and cancelling?
Travel subscriptions usually rely on:
  • Minimum membership terms (e.g. 3–6 months before use).
  • Trip credits that accumulate over time before redemption.
  • Loyalty rewards for long-term members.
  • Pause options instead of full cancellation.
Those same ideas can be built into FlyLoop’s model.
Can this model be profitable?
Profit comes from negotiated rates, group departures, breakage (not everyone uses all trips), and upsells like extra nights, upgrades or tours. This FAQ can be expanded with financial modeling for pitches.
